Welcome to MegaTech Basic Chip Level Training!

Are you ready to embark on a transformative journey into the heart of laptop motherboard repair? Join us for an immersive 3-week training program that will equip you with the essential skills needed to excel in the world of electronics troubleshooting.

📅 Training Schedule:

Week 1: Dive into the realm of Surface Mount Device (SMD) components, including capacitors, diodes, resistors, fuses, transistors, and MOSFETs. Understand their functions and learn how to identify them on a laptop motherboard. Delve deeper into MOSFETs, discovering how they serve as switches and understanding their source and drain pins.

Week 2: Explore complex Integrated Circuits (ICs) such as inductors, super IO chips, PWM chips, and step-down ICs. Gain insights into their roles and functionalities within the motherboard architecture.

Week 3: Engage in a comprehensive video course focusing on multimeter usage for troubleshooting. Learn the major modes of testing, including continuity, resistance, voltage, and diode modes.

🔍 Course Highlights:

Continuity Mode Testing: Master the theory and practical application of continuity testing, SMD MOSFET testing, and ceramic capacitor analysis.

Resistance Mode Testing: Decode through-hole and SMD resistor color coding, learn practical testing methods, and understand multimeter setup for resistance mode.

Voltage Mode Testing: Gain expertise in setting up your multimeter for voltage testing, reading laptop charger outputs, tracking motherboard voltage, and basic motherboard repair.

Diode Mode Testing: Learn everything about diodes and how to effectively test them for functionality.
